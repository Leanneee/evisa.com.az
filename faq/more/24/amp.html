What will happen if I apply for standard processing e-Visa less than 3 business days before my departure from my country?

In this case we will contact you by email or phone and advice you two below mentioned options:

Option 1: To change the arrival date in Azerbaijan.
Option 2: To change the processing type to urgent visa type. Normally urgent processing e-Visa type is expensive than standard processing. 

Please, be aware that if we are not able to get your decision about above mentioned two options by email or phone, we will begin the process for standard e-Visa. However, we cannot guarantee the visa will be ready on time as you applied for visa less than 3 business days before your departure from your country.
